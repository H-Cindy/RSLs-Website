Meetings

To get involved in the club, the best way is to come to a meeting! We meet weekly on Mondays from 5 - 6 PM in Ives 217 (enter through the entrance off of Tower Rd. and make a left) during the academic year, not including breaks.

You should also join our Slack for general communication and updates.

Weekly emails are sent before each g-body meeting on Mondays. Be sure to fill out the survey to ensure you're on the list.

Follow our Instagram account @cornell_rsls to get stay connected.
